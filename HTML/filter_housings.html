Filter Housings and Filter Cartridges

Filter Housings and Filter Cartridges

At Arfath Global Enterprises, we specialize in supplying premium-quality filter housings and filter cartridges engineered to meet the stringent filtration requirements of diverse industrial sectors. Whether you're in pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, food & beverage processing, power generation, or water and wastewater treatment, our filtration solutions are designed to deliver superior performance, reliability, and durability.

Filter Housings

Our filter housings are manufactured from robust materials such as stainless steel (304/316L), polypropylene, PVDF, and other corrosion-resistant alloys, ensuring long service life and high chemical compatibility. They are available in various configurations to accommodate:

  • Single-round and multi-round cartridges
  • High-flow and high-capacity designs
  • Sanitary and industrial-grade applications
  • Pressure ratings up to 150 psi or more
  • Custom sizes and connections (flanged, threaded, tri-clamp, etc.)

These housings are designed for easy maintenance, tool-free cartridge replacement, and secure sealing, making them ideal for high-purity and critical process applications.

Filter Cartridges

Our filter cartridges come in a variety of media types, micron ratings, and construction styles to suit a wide range of filtration needs:

  • Depth filters (melt-blown, spun-bonded)
  • Pleated filters for extended surface area and higher flow rates
  • Activated carbon filters for taste, odor, and chemical reduction
  • Membrane filters (PES, PTFE, PVDF, Nylon) for microbial retention and fine filtration
  • Resin-bonded, string-wound, and washable cartridges for cost-effective use in heavy-duty applications

Available in micron ratings from 0.1 µm to 100 µm, our cartridges provide high dirt- holding capacity, low-pressure drop, and consistent filtration efficiency.

Filter Housings

PVC Bag Filter Housings

Our PVC Bag Filter Housings are engineered for cost-effective, chemical-resistant filtration. Manufactured using high-grade polyvinyl chloride (PVC), these housings are non-corrosive, lightweight, and suitable for a wide range of acidic and alkaline environments.

Key features:

  • Resistant to corrosion, acids, and alkalis
  • Compatible with industry-standard bag sizes (Supports Ø 7” × 16” and Ø 7” × 32” Filter Bags.)
  • Tool-free access for fast bag changes
  • Low-pressure drop across housing

Typical Applications:

  • Electroplating and metal finishing
  • Pre-treatment in water and wastewater plants
  • Industrial chemical filtration
  • Paints, coatings, and ink manufacturing

Cartridge Filter Housings

We offer Cartridge Filter Housings in various materials including stainless steel (SS304/SS316), polypropylene (PP), PVC and Carbon steel, accommodating multiple cartridge configurations such as DOE, 222/fin, 226/flat and more.

Key features:

  • Single or multi-round designs (1 to 40 cartridges)
  • Pressure ratings up to 10 bar / 150 PSI
  • Sanitary options available for FDA-grade applications
  • Vent and drain ports for complete draining and ease of maintenance
  • ASME-code or non-code models available

Typical Applications:

  • Pharmaceutical-grade sterile filtration
  • Beverage and dairy processing
  • Reverse osmosis (RO) system pre-filtration
  • Chemical process streams
  • Cooling tower and boiler feedwater

Filter Cartridges

Depth-Type Filter Cartridges

Spun Bonded Filter Cartridges

Made from gradient-density 100% polypropylene fibers, these filters capture a wide spectrum of particulate sizes from coarse to fine throughout their entire depth.

Benefits:

  • High dirt-holding capacity
  • Nominal and absolute rated versions
  • Thermally bonded, no binders or adhesives
  • Micron ratings: 0.5 to 100 µm

Applications: RO pre-filtration, potable water, inks, emulsions, and oil removal

Wound Filter Cartridges

These are precision-wound using polypropylene, cotton, or glass fiber yarn for mechanical strength and chemical compatibility.

Benefits:

  • Long service life in high-sediment environments
  • Core materials: stainless steel, polypropylene, or tin
  • Handles high temperatures Up to 200°C (varies based on material)

Applications: Solvent filtration, edible oils, resins, deionized water, brines

Resin Bonded Filter Cartridges

Constructed using thermosetting resin-bonded fibers that offer exceptional strength, temperature, and chemical resistance.

Benefits:

  • Rigid structure prevents unloading
  • Temperature range: up to 120°C
  • Excellent for viscous fluids and oils

Applications: Hydraulic oils, lubricants, fuels, petrochemicals, metalworking fluids

Pleated Filter Cartridges (Surface Filtration)

Polypropylene Pleated Filter Cartridges

Precision-molded cartridges with a pleated polypropylene media for high-efficiency particle removal and extended life.

Benefits:

  • Absolute micron ratings from 0.1 to 100 µm
  • Up to 3x more surface area than depth filters
  • Compatible with sanitizing agents and steam

Applications: Hydraulic oils, lubricants, fuels, petrochemicals, metalworking fluids

PTFE Filter Cartridges

Made from hydrophobic polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) membrane, ideal for aggressive chemical and gas filtration.

Benefits:

  • Excellent chemical and thermal resistance
  • Withstands sterilization cycles
  • Available in both hydrophobic and hydrophilic versions

Applications: Corrosive solvents, sterile vent filtration, compressed gases, electronics

PES Filter Cartridges

These use polyethersulfone (PES) membranes for high throughput and low protein binding in critical liquid applications.

Benefits:

  • Biologically inert
  • High flow rates with low pressure drops
  • Broad pH compatibility (3–10)

Applications: Injectables, vaccines, fermentation broths, diagnostics

Specialty Filter Cartridges

Activated Carbon Filter Cartridges

Incorporating premium-grade carbon granules or carbon-impregnated media for efficient adsorption of chlorine, VOCs, and bad taste/odors.

Benefits:

  • Removes organics, chlorine, THMs
  • Enhances taste and odor quality
  • Long life and low pressure drop

Applications: Drinking water, food processing, cosmetics, and brewing

Oil Adsorbing Filter Cartridges

Designed for efficient oil removal using a multi-layer absorbent structure that captures dispersed and emulsified hydrocarbons.

Benefits:

  • Removes oil at ppm and ppb levels
  • High retention capacity
  • Cost-effective alternative to traditional oil separators

Applications: Wash water, wastewater polishing, metal cleaning, machining

High Flowment Filter Cartridges

Large-diameter, pleated cartridges for high-flow applications, offering low pressure loss and reduced footprint.

Benefits:

  • Flow rates up to 500 GPM per cartridge
  • Outer cage prevents pleat damage
  • Available in vertical or horizontal configurations

Applications: Power generation, seawater intake, cooling circuits, pre-RO

Rigid/Sintered Filter Cartridges

Stainless Steel Filter Cartridges

Reusable sintered or mesh SS cartridges withstand extreme temperatures, chemicals, and pressures.

Benefits:

  • Withstands >300°C temperatures
  • Micron ratings from 0.5 to 100 µm
  • Easy backwash and clean-in-place (CIP)

Applications: Steam sterilization, hot oils, aggressive chemical filtration

Sintered (Non-Metallic) Filter Cartridges

Made from sintered ceramic, polyethylene, or PTFE for precision filtration in specialty chemical and pharmaceutical applications.

Benefits:

  • Inert to most acids, alkalis, and solvents
  • Rigid and stable under extreme conditions
  • Washable and reusable

Applications: Acid filtration, sterile air/gas, final polishing of chemicals
