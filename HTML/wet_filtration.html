Wet Filtration Solutions

Arfath Global provides advanced wet filtration solutions for industries like chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food & beverage, and wastewater treatment. Our filters ensure reliable, efficient performance, keeping your processes optimal and cost-effective.

Our Filtration Products

We offer a wide range of filter fabrics and technical textiles, including filter bags, cloths, panels, and screens. These are designed for various applications and equipment types.

Our Expertise

We deliver optimal liquid filtration solutions using materials like polypropylene, polyester, nylon, and PTFE. These fibers are selected for their durability and performance in industrial settings.

Fabric Types

  • Multifilament by Multifilament: Strong, durable, and ideal for high-pressure environments.
  • Staple by Staple: Excellent for fine particle retention, perfect for high-purity needs.
  • Monofilament by Multifilament: Combines strength and fine particle retention for versatile filtration.
  • Multifilament by Staple Fiber: Robust and effective at particle capture and sludge removal.

Key Features

  • Chemical Compatibility: Withstands aggressive solvents and a wide pH range.
  • High-Temperature Resistance: Performs well under elevated temperatures.
  • Pressure Tolerance: Tested for high-pressure applications to prevent rupture.
  • Precise Particle Retention: Advanced weaving for consistent filtration.
  • Easy Maintenance: Simple to clean, reducing downtime and improving efficiency.

Explore Our Full Range of Wet Filtration Solutions

Filter Bags

Achieve optimal filtration performance with our diverse range of filter bags, designed for efficient separation of solids from liquids and slurries. Whether for general industrial use or highly specific applications, our filter bags offer dependable and customizable solutions.

Sewn / Welded Filter Bags

Engineered for durability and leak-proof performance, these bags come in both sewn and welded variants to meet diverse industrial requirements.

  • Application: Liquid and slurry filtration in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.
  • Materials: Polyester, Polypropylene, Nylon.
  • Advantages: Welded seams for superior strength, leak-free operation, and customizable sizes.

Felt Filter Bags

Felt filter bags are built for high-performance filtration where large volumes of particulate matter need to be captured without sacrificing flow rate.

  • Application: Ideal for high dirt-holding capacity, used in pharmaceutical, chemical, and food processing.
  • Materials: Felted synthetic fabrics like Polyester and Polypropylene.
  • Advantages: High dirt-holding capacity, excellent flow rates, and durability under pressure.

Oil Absorbing Bags

Specially designed to target and remove oils from various liquids, these bags play a crucial role in environmental protection and industrial cleanliness.

  • Application: Removes oil from water in automotive, oil refining, and wastewater treatment.
  • Materials: Oil-absorbing nonwoven fabrics.
  • Advantages: High oil absorption, effective in wastewater treatment, and prevents environmental contamination.

Water Absorbent Filter Bags

A perfect solution for handling water-based contaminants, these filter bags help maintain process stability and protect sensitive operations.

  • Application: Water-soluble contaminant filtration for chemical and pharmaceutical industries.
  • Materials: Synthetic materials with high absorbency properties.
  • Advantages: Ideal for absorbing water-based contaminants, reducing operational issues in sensitive processes.

PP Pleated Hardshell Bags

Designed for high-volume liquid processing, the pleated hardshell structure maximizes surface area and minimizes downtime.

  • Application: High-flow filtration in applications requiring large volumes of liquid processing.
  • Materials: Polypropylene (PP) with pleated design.
  • Advantages: Increased surface area, higher flow rates, and reduced maintenance costs.

Centrifuge Bags

Made for demanding centrifuge environments, these bags offer high resistance and efficiency in separating solids from liquids under extreme conditions.

  • Application: Solid-liquid separation in centrifuges used in biotechnology, food processing, and chemical industries.
  • Materials: Strong polypropylene or polyester.
  • Advantages: High centrifugal force resistance, durable, long-lasting filtration performance.

Nutsche Filter Bags

Perfect for vacuum or pressure filtration systems, Nutsche filter bags provide consistent separation performance in critical processes.

  • Application: Solid-liquid filtration under pressure, used in chemical and pharmaceutical applications.
  • Materials: Polypropylene, polyester, or special composites.
  • Advantages: Excellent filtration under pressure, high dirt-holding capacity, and efficient separation.

Disc Filter Bags

Engineered for use in disc filtration systems, these filter bags support large-scale, high- efficiency operations across heavy-duty sectors.

  • Application: Used in disc filtration systems, commonly in mining, chemical, and wastewater treatment.
  • Materials: Nonwoven fabrics or polyester.
  • Advantages: High filtration efficiency, ideal for large-scale filtration systems.

Fluid Bed Dryer (FBD) Bags

Designed for heat-intensive environments, FBD bags ensure efficient drying of fine particles while maintaining cleanliness and product integrity.

  • Application: Used in fluid bed dryers for drying powders, granules, and other materials in pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food industries.
  • Materials: Polyester, nylon, or other high-temperature resistant fabrics.
  • Advantages: Resistant to heat, durable, and efficient in preventing dust and particles from contaminating the product.

NMO Filter Bags

These general-purpose filter bags provide a balanced mix of performance and affordability for a wide range of liquid filtration needs.

  • Application: General-purpose filtration for industries like water treatment and food processing.
  • Materials: Nonwoven materials.
  • Advantages: Versatile, cost-effective, and efficient in various filtration environments.

Filter Cloth

Our high-performance filter cloths are crafted to meet the demanding needs of pressure and vacuum filtration systems. Designed for long-term use, they ensure consistent filtration efficiency in harsh operational environments.

Filter Press Cloth

Built for use in demanding industrial processes, these cloths deliver effective solid-liquid separation in high-pressure systems.

  • Application: Used in filter presses for separating liquids and solids in chemical, pharmaceutical, and mineral processing.
  • Materials: Polyester, polypropylene, and cotton.
  • Advantages: High resistance to wear and chemicals, excellent filtration efficiency, and durable under high pressure.

Rotary Vacuum Drum Filter Cloth

Ideal for continuous filtration processes, these cloths ensure smooth operation and minimized build-up, increasing throughput and reducing maintenance.

  • Application: Continuous filtration in rotary vacuum drum filters, commonly used in wastewater treatment, mining, and food production.
  • Materials: Polypropylene, polyester, or composite fabrics.
  • Advantages: Smooth surface, reduces cake build-up, and offers excellent filtration for continuous processes.

Belt Filter Cloth

Engineered for superior dewatering, these cloths are the workhorses of the filtration world, especially where high throughput is essential.

  • Application: Dewatering in mining, pulp and paper, and food processing industries.
  • Materials: Polyester, polypropylene, or synthetic fibers.
  • Advantages: High wear resistance, long service life, and high capacity for solid- liquid separation.

Filter Pads

Designed for clarity and consistency, our filter pads offer cost-effective solutions for batch and small-scale filtration needs in quality-sensitive industries.

Sparkler Filter Pads

These pads are a trusted choice for fine filtration of liquids where purity and clarity are essential, especially in pharmaceutical and food-grade operations.

  • Application: Used in sparkler filters to clarify liquids, including oils, beverages, and pharmaceuticals.
  • Materials: Cellulose, synthetic fibers.
  • Advantages: High dirt-holding capacity, fine filtration, and easy to change out, making it perfect for small-scale batch filtration.

Specialty Filter Media

For operations that require customized and precise filtration, our specialty media are engineered to deliver targeted results with consistent quality.

Coolant Filter Paper Rolls

Essential for maintaining fluid cleanliness and operational efficiency, these rolls are perfect for extending the service life of machinery and reducing downtime.

  • Application: Filtration of coolant liquids in automotive, machinery, and metalworking industries.
  • Materials: High-quality filter paper.
  • Advantages: Excellent particle retention, extends the life of coolants, and maintains system efficiency.
