Dust Collection Bags & Filters

Our collection of dust collection bags and filters is engineered to meet the specific needs of industrial operations, offering superior filtration performance, durability, and versatility across a wide range of applications.

Pleated Dust Collection Bags

Pleated dust collection bags are designed for high-efficiency dust filtration, especially in applications with high air volumes. Their pleated design increases the surface area, allowing for better dust collection and a longer service life. These bags are ideal for industries like cement, wood processing, food production, and pharmaceuticals, where large volumes of dust need to be captured.

  • Applications: Cement manufacturing, grain mills, woodworking shops, pharmaceutical production
  • Advantages: Improved filtration, reduced maintenance, longer lifespan

Polyester Dust Collection Bags

Polyester dust collection bags are highly versatile and cost-effective. Made from durable polyester material, these bags provide excellent resistance to abrasion, moisture, and chemical exposure. They are ideal for general-purpose dust collection in various industrial environments.

  • Applications: Food processing, paper mills, metalworking
  • Advantages: Cost-effective, long-lasting, resistant to abrasion

Aramid - Nomex Filter Bags

Aramid or Nomex filter bags are designed for high-temperature environments, providing superior resistance to heat, chemical exposure, and wear. They are perfect for industries where heat resistance is a critical factor, such as in metal smelting, asphalt production, and cement plants.

  • Applications: High-temperature applications, metalworking, power plants
  • Applications: High-temperature applications, metalworking, power plants

P84 Filter Bags

P84 filter bags are made from a high-performance fiber that offers exceptional filtration at elevated temperatures. These bags excel in applications where high-efficiency filtration of fine particulate matter is needed, such as in power plants and chemical processing.

  • Applications: High-temperature filtration, industrial air purification, chemical processing
  • Advantages: High thermal stability, fine particle capture, long service life

Homopolymer Acrylic Needle Felt Filter Bags

These bags are made from needle-felted acrylic fibers, providing a high level of filtration for medium-temperature applications. They offer excellent dust capture efficiency and are used in industries that require moderate temperature resistance.

  • Applications: Textile, tobacco, and general industrial applications
  • Advantages: Affordable, durable, and efficient for mid-range temperatures

Woven Fiber Glass Filter Bags

Woven fiberglass filter bags are known for their robustness and ability to withstand high temperatures and harsh chemicals. They provide a long-lasting filtration solution for industries dealing with high-temperature exhaust systems.

  • Applications: High-temperature dust collection, chemical plants, incinerators
  • Advantages: High heat resistance, long operational life

Non-Woven Fiber Glass Filter Bags

Non-woven fiberglass bags are designed for industries with demanding dust loads and where high filtration efficiency is essential. Their non-woven structure allows for a high dust holding capacity, making them ideal for applications with high particulate volumes.

  • Applications: Cement plants, steel mills, power plants
  • Advantages: High dust holding capacity, excellent filtration efficiency

Vacuum Filter Bags

Vacuum filter bags are essential for efficient dust collection in vacuum-based systems. They are often used in industries where a high degree of cleanliness is required, such as in pharmaceutical or food processing plants.

  • Applications: Pharmaceutical, food processing, electronic manufacturing
  • Advantages: Enhanced filtration, ease of use in vacuum systems

Shaker Filter Bags

Shaker filter bags are designed for industrial shaker systems that rely on mechanical vibration for cleaning. These bags allow for easy maintenance and provide consistent filtration even in systems that experience frequent cleaning cycles.

  • Applications: Cement, mining, and food processing industries
  • Advantages: Easy maintenance, consistent filtration during frequent cleaning

Pulse-Jet / Reverse Pulse Jet Air Dust Collection Bags

Pulse-jet filter bags are specially designed for systems that utilize high-pressure air to periodically clean the filters. They are efficient for high dust-loading applications, providing maximum filtration with minimal downtime for cleaning.

  • Applications: Power plants, cement production, chemical industries
  • Advantages: High filtration efficiency, minimal downtime, superior dust removal

Specialized Filter Bags & Connectors

Plan Sifter Sleeves

Plan sifter sleeves are custom-made to fit plan sifters used in flour mills and other grain- processing industries. These specialized sleeves help in fine particulate separation, ensuring minimal dust escape and cleaner air in processing facilities.

  • Applications: Flour mills, grain processing
  • Advantages: Enhanced dust collection, easy installation, and long-lasting performance

Pulverizer Sleeves

Pulverizer sleeves are designed to handle high dust loads generated during material grinding processes. They provide efficient filtration in pulverizing systems, preventing contamination and improving air quality.

  • Applications: Cement, coal grinding, and powder processing
  • Advantages: Increased filtration efficiency, long service life

Flexible Fabric Bellow Connectors

Flexible fabric bellow connectors are designed to absorb vibrations and movements in air handling systems. These connectors improve the stability and longevity of filtration systems while providing a secure and flexible seal between components.

  • Applications: Air ducts, filtration units, pneumatic systems
  • Advantages: Vibration absorption, improved system longevity, secure sealing

Industrial Air Filtration Systems

Our industrial air filtration systems provide large-scale solutions for businesses that need reliable and high-performance filtration for their operations.

Reverse Air Bag Houses

Reverse air bag houses use a reverse air flow mechanism to clean the filter bags. These systems are designed for continuous operation in industrial settings where downtime for filter cleaning must be minimized. They are ideal for environments with high dust loads and provide long-term durability.

  • Applications: Cement plants, coal-fired power plants, steel mills
  • AAdvantages: Minimal downtime, effective dust control, energy efficiency

HVAC Filter Panels

HVAC filter panels are used in ventilation and air-conditioning systems to ensure high air quality in commercial, industrial, and residential buildings. These filters capture dust, allergens, and other contaminants, contributing to healthier indoor air environments.

  • Applications: Commercial buildings, hospitals, data centers
  • Advantages: Improved indoor air quality, high filtration efficiency

Gas Turbine Air Intake Filter Cartridges

These filter cartridges are designed to protect gas turbines from contaminants that can reduce performance and lifespan. Made with high-quality materials, they ensure that turbines operate efficiently by filtering out particulate matter and debris from the air intake.

  • Applications: Power plants, industrial generators, offshore platforms
  • Advantages: Protects turbines, improves performance, extends service life

High-Efficiency Filtration

For environments that require ultra-pure air, we offer high-efficiency filtration solutions that capture even the smallest airborne contaminants.

HEPA Filter Panels

HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filter panels are capable of removing 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them ideal for applications where clean air is critical, such as in clean rooms, hospitals, and pharmaceuticals.

  • Applications: Clean rooms, pharmaceuticals, hospitals, electronic manufacturing
  • Advantages: High particle removal efficiency, ideal for clean environments

Mesh Filter Bags

Mesh filter bags are typically used for high-flow dust collection systems and are known for their durability and long service life. These bags are ideal for capturing coarse particles and ensuring efficient airflow without clogging.

  • Applications: Bulk material handling, mining, chemical processing
  • Advantages: High-flow filtration, reduced risk of clogging, durable

Cartridge Filters

Cartridge filters offer compact, efficient filtration solutions for various industrial applications. They provide high filtration capacity and long operational life, ensuring effective dust and particulate control.

Pleated Cartridge Filters

Pleated cartridge filters are designed with pleated media to maximize surface area and capture fine particulates in air and liquid filtration systems. Their compact design and easy installation make them ideal for industries requiring efficient dust control in small spaces.

  • Applications: Food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, air handling systems
  • Advantages: Increased surface area, easy installation, long life
