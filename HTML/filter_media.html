Filter Media

High-Performance Filter Media Tailored to Your Needs

At Arfath Global Enterprises, we offer one of India’s widest selections of filtration and separation fabrics. Our products include woven and non-woven filter cloths, technical textiles, filter bags, filter panels, and expert filtration services. We're not just fabric suppliers—we provide complete filtration solutions, earning trust and market leadership. We're also among the largest stockists of filter fabrics in India.

Expertise in Fibre Properties

We design our products to match complex industrial needs by studying the chemical, thermal, and mechanical properties of fibres. Our deep understanding of fibre-to-fabric processing ensures high filter performance.

Our Filter Media Range:

  • Woven Filter Cloth: Available in various fibre types and yarns (monofilament, spun, multifilament), weave patterns, and finishes for better filtration, cake formation, and discharge.
  • Non-Woven Filter Cloth: Made using needle-felt and adhesive bonding in fibres like cotton, polyester, polypropylene, Nomex, Ryton, and more. Finishes include PTFE coatings, antistatic, fire retardant, and others for enhanced durability and chemical resistance.
  • Performance Benefits: Our filter media offer durability, reliability, optimal flow, easy cake removal, reusability, and excellent chemical compatibility—always clean and contaminant- free.

Explore our Full Range of Filter Media

Woven Filter Cloths

Our woven filter cloths are crafted with precision to deliver outstanding filtration performance across a wide range of industrial applications. Whether you're dealing with light dust collection or aggressive chemical slurries, our fabric solutions provide durability, chemical resistance, and consistent filtration accuracy.

Cotton Filter Cloth

A natural filtration solution ideal for moisture-laden or organic environments.

  • Material: 100% natural cotton fibers
  • Applications: Food processing, textile manufacturing, light industrial filtration, moisture removal
  • Advantages: Excellent breathability, biodegradable, high moisture absorption, eco- friendly

Nylon Filter Cloth

Engineered for high-performance filtration where strength and durability are critical.

  • Material: Polyamide (nylon) fibers
  • Applications: Chemical processing, water treatment, heavy-duty industrial filtration
  • Advantages: High tensile strength, abrasion resistance, elastic recovery, excellent chemical stability

Polyester Filter Cloth

A robust, all-purpose filter cloth with exceptional chemical and UV resistance.

  • Material: Polyethylene terephthalate (PET)
  • Applications: Dust filtration, chemical and food industries, centrifuge systems
  • Advantages: Chemical inertness, UV resistance, microbial resistance, dimensional stability

Polypropylene Filter Cloth

Lightweight and hydrophobic, this fabric is ideal for acidic and alkaline environments.

  • Material: Polypropylene fibers
  • Applications: Water treatment, oil and grease filtration, chemical slurry handling
  • Advantages: Strong chemical resistance, low moisture absorption, cost-effective

Polyethylene Woven Filter Cloth

A lightweight, strong option for filtering harsh chemicals and outdoor exposure.

  • Material: High-density polyethylene (HDPE)
  • Applications: Acid and alkali filtration, geotextile drainage, outdoor filtration systems
  • Advantages: Corrosion resistance, mechanical durability, UV stable

Fiberglass Woven Filter Cloth

Designed for extreme temperature applications in high-heat and corrosive conditions.

  • Material: Woven fiberglass yarns
  • Applications: Exhaust gas filtration, kilns, thermal and furnace systems
  • Advantages: Heat resistance up to 500°C (932°F), corrosion-resistant, stable under thermal stress

Stainless Steel Woven Filter Fabric

The gold standard for mechanical strength and precision in challenging filtration conditions.

  • Material: Stainless steel wire mesh (grades SS304, SS316, and SS316L)
  • Applications: Food and beverage filtration, metalworking fluids, high-pressure filtration
  • Advantages: High temperature tolerance, corrosion-resistant, reusable, exact micron control

PTFE Woven Filter Fabric

Ideal for aggressive chemical environments and non-stick applications.

  • Material: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) fibers
  • Applications: Chemical processing, sticky or corrosive filtrates, pharmaceuticals
  • Advantages: Chemically inert, non-stick surface, withstands up to 260°C (500°F).

Aramid (Nomex) Woven Filter Fabric

A heat-resistant fabric suitable for high-temperature industrial operations.

  • Material: Aramid fibers (e.g., Nomex)
  • Applications: Cement plants, asphalt production, power plants
  • Advantages: Flame retardant, excellent thermal and mechanical strength, long service life

PVDF Woven Filter Fabric

An advanced fabric for chemically aggressive and UV-exposed environments.

  • Material: Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) fibers
  • Applications: Wastewater treatment, chemical filtration, outdoor filtration systems
  • Advantages: Exceptional chemical resistance, UV stable, durable in extreme environments

Specialty Fabrics (likely Non-Woven or Specialty)

Our specialty filter fabrics address unique performance challenges such as abrasion resistance, high tensile strength, and precision filtration. These materials support specialized industries where conventional fabrics fall short.

Nylon Dipped Fabrics

A coated fabric solution for applications where exposure to water and chemicals is common.

  • Material: Nylon fabric with dip coating (PU/PVC or other polymers)
  • Applications: Industrial filtration, flexible connectors, abrasion-prone areas
  • Advantages: Water-resistant, durable, extended service life in harsh conditions

Nylon Mesh Cloth

A lightweight mesh fabric for precision sieving and fine particulate separation.

  • Material: Woven nylon fibers
  • Applications: Food and pharma filtration, laboratory sieving, electronics
  • Advantages: High permeability, flexible, strong tensile strength, customizable mesh openings

Carbon Fiber Fabric

Used where extreme strength and thermal stability are necessary alongside minimal weight.

  • Material: Carbon fibers woven into fabric
  • Applications: High-strength filtration, composites, heat shielding
  • Advantages: Superior strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion-resistant, thermally stable
