Food Processing

Applications: Edible Oils, Beverages, Dairy, Bakery, and Flour Mills

  • All filter media are food-grade, ensuring no contamination while delivering top-notch filtration performance.
  • Media types include: candle filter bags, centrifuges, filter press fabrics, vacuum belt filters, screen meshes, vessel bags, and more.
  • Key processes supported:
    • Dairy & powdered milk production
    • Brewing, juice & soft drink processing
    • Sugar refining, starch extraction, oil purification
    • Biscuit and bakery production
  • Fabric properties offered: burst resistance, optimal filtration rate, heat resistance, and clean cake discharge.
  • Trusted partner in edible oil and biscuit manufacturing sectors.

Life Sciences & Pharmaceuticals

  • Designed for high-throughput production with contaminant-free filter media.
  • Applications include: antibiotics, vaccines, plasma products, and synthetic medicines.
  • Chemical filtration: organic/inorganic pigments, iron & titanium oxides, plastics, detergents.
  • Equipment compatibility: centrifuge bags, Nutsche filters, filter press cloths, fluid bed dryer fabrics, drum/disc filters, and sparkler pads.
  • Our media support particle separations from 0.5 to 500 µm, even under extreme temperatures up to 200°C.
